Cantor Lecture Theatre

Our Lecture Theatre

The Cantor Building, home to our arts and computing students, is a modern and sophisticated city centre venue just a few minutes' walk from Sheffield railway station.

This spacious double height lecture theatre has tiered seating and state-of-the-art audiovisual facilities. There is a static lectern, which has a microphone built-in, with capacity for an additional four microphones to be added.

Cantor Atrium

Our Atrium

The Cantor Atrium is at the heart of the Cantor Building, just one floor down from the Cantor Lecture Theatre, which can be easily reached by stairs or via lifts.

The Cantor Atrium is a light and airy venue which can be used for delegate registration, exhibitions, drinks receptions, informal presentations and as a catering and breakout space.
